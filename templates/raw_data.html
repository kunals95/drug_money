Raw Data

Below you will find the more than 30 GB of raw datasets I utilized for my analysis.

Open Payments

4 datasets (1 per year) - Disclose payments healthcare providers received by pharmaceutical companies & device manufacturers for gifts, travel, meals, licensing, research, etc.

Medicare Part D Prescriber Data

4 datasets (1 per year) - Outline the prescription drugs prescribed by healthcare providers that were paid for under the Medicare Part D Prescription Drug Program.

National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES)

1 dataset - Contains all active National Provider Identifier (NPI) records.

National Drug Code Directory

1 dataset - Provides a list of all the current pharmaceutical drugs that are on sale for consumers.

Product Data for Drugs in Medicaid Drug Rebate Program

1 dataset - Provides a list of all the active pharmaceutical drugs that have been reported by drug manufacturers.


All datasets were obtained through the United States Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services website: cms.gov.
